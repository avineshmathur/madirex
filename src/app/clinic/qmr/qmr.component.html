QME Appointment Notification Form
Please complete this form in its entirety. The Administrative Director requires that you serve this appointment notification form on the employee and the claims administrator, or, if none the employer, and their attorneys in a represented case, if known, within five (5) business days after having scheduled the injured worker to be seen for a QME comprehensive medical-legal evaluation. You may not cancel the appointment less than six (6) calendar days prior to the appointment date, except for good cause (See, 8 Cal. Code Regs. §34). If you reschedule an appointment, review regulation 34 and the ethical rules in regulation 41 (See, 8 Cal Code Regs. §§ 34, 41(a) (7) and (a) (8)).
